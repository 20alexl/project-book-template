# Chapter 0 — The Elevator Pitch

[← Back to Table of Contents](./README.md) · [Next: The Problem →](./01-the-problem.md)

---

> Explain this research like you're at a poster session and someone just walked up.
> No jargon. No equations. Just the core idea.

## What is this research about?

*2-3 sentences. What are you studying, what's the approach, and what did you find?*

## Why should anyone care?

*What's the practical or theoretical impact? Who benefits from this work?*

## The One-Liner

> *If you had to describe this research in a single sentence for a conference badge, what would it say?*
