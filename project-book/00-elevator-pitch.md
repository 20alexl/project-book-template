# Chapter 0 — The Elevator Pitch

[← Back to Table of Contents](./README.md) · [Next: The Why →](./01-the-why.md)

---

> Write this chapter as if someone asked "so what does this thing do?" at a coffee shop.
> One page. No jargon. If someone reads nothing else, they read this.

## What is {Project Name}?

*2-3 sentences. Plain English. What does it do and why should anyone care?*

## Who is it for?

*Who are the users? Be specific — not "developers" but "backend engineers who need to process payment webhooks without managing queue infrastructure."*

## What does it replace?

*What were people doing before this existed? Manual process? Competitor tool? Nothing?*

## The One-Liner

> *If you had to describe this project in a single sentence on a conference badge, what would it say?*
